Potential Affirmative Contentions


  • Economic Recovery and Growth: Rejoining gives the UK access to the single market, boosting trade and investment.

  • Global Influence: The UK regains a major voice in EU policymaking, strengthening diplomatic ties.

  • Human Rights Protections: EU membership ensures strong legal protections for labor, environment, and privacy.

  • Public and Democratic Support: Shifting public opinion, especially among young people, suggests rejoining may reflect the will of the people.

  • Northern Ireland Peace: Rejoining helps stabilize the Irish border and supports the Good Friday Agreement.
