Participants in this Debate


UK Government and Parliament

Would be responsible for making and passing legislation if the UK were to rejoin the EU.


European Union

The EU (especially the European Commission and European Parliament) would have to accept the UK back under negotiated terms.


British Political Parties

  • Labour Party: Mixed views, but many support stronger EU ties
  • Conservative Party: Led Brexit; largely against rejoining
  • Liberal Democrats & SNP: Strongly pro-EU and in favor of rejoining

British Citizens

Public opinion is shifting, especially among younger generations who are usually be more pro-EU.


Trade & Business Leaders

Many in the business community favor rejoining to restore economic predictability and trade access.


EU Member States

Some support the idea of the UK rejoining; others are cautious or indifferent, depending on past Brexit tensions and current national interests.
