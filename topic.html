Resolution


Resolved: The United Kingdom should rejoin the European Union.

This is a policy resolution, meaning the affirmative must support taking an action â€” rejoining the EU.


Evaluation Criteria


  • Actor: The United Kingdom (specifically, its government)
  • Action: Rejoining the European Union

Affirmative Burden


  • Must prove that the UK ought to rejoin the EU
  • Must show that the benefits outweigh the harms

Negative Burden


  • Can argue that the UK should not rejoin
  • Or argue that better alternatives exist
  • NEG positions may include:
    • Brexit outcomes have benefits worth keeping
    • Rejoining is impractical or politically unrealistic
    • The UK could pursue closer ties without full membership
